Choccolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingreident

  • 1 Cup Butter orMargarine, Softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 Cup Brown Sugar, Packed
  • Eggs
  • 2 teaspoons Vanilla
  • 1/4 Cups flour, Unsifted
  • 1 teaspoon Baking powder
  • 1 cup Walnuts or pecans
  • 2 cups KCC Bittersweet

Baking Instruction

  1. Heat Oven to 375F
  2. Stir flour with baking soda and salt; set aside.
  3. In large mixing bowl, beat butter with sugar, and brown sugar
  4. Add eggs and Vanilla, one at a time.
    Mix on low speed
  5. Gradually blend dry mixture into creamed mixture.
    stir.
  6. Drop by tablespoon onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until chocolate chip cookies
  8. Enjoy
